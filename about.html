About Andrew L. Nash, MD

30+ years of pediatric experience dedicated to your child's wellbeing

Meet Andrew L. Nash, MD FAAP

With over 30 years of experience practicing primary care pediatrics, Andrew L. Nash, MD has dedicated his career to providing exceptional, personalized care for children and families.

Professional Journey

Dr. Nash founded 1-to-1 Pediatrics in 2011 to provide personalized, "hometown" style pediatric care for families in the San Francisco East Bay Area. His vision was to create a practice where every child receives individualized attention and comprehensive care that goes beyond traditional pediatric medicine.

Responding to Growing Needs

Since starting his unique practice, Dr. Nash has witnessed a startling increase in the incidence of anxiety and related disorders in the pediatric population. The COVID-19 pandemic exacerbated this trend, leading to exponential growth in ADHD and anxiety cases among children and adolescents.

New Chapter: Specialized Care

Recognizing this critical need, Dr. Nash is expanding his practice to offer specialized diagnosis and management of ADHD and anxiety-related disorders through a secure telemedicine platform starting in 2026. This new service allows him to extend his expertise and compassionate care to families throughout California.

Our Treatment Philosophy

Child-Centered Care

Every child is unique. We develop individualized treatment plans that consider your child's specific needs, strengths, and challenges.

Family Partnership

We work collaboratively with parents, caregivers, and families to ensure comprehensive support and consistent care across all environments.

Evidence-Based Approach

Our treatment methods are grounded in the latest research and clinical evidence for ADHD and anxiety management in children and adolescents.

Educational Support

We help children succeed academically by working with schools and providing strategies that support learning and focus in educational settings.

Areas of Expertise

ADHD Diagnosis & Management

Comprehensive evaluation for attention deficit hyperactivity disorder, including assessment of inattentive, hyperactive-impulsive, and combined presentations.

Anxiety Disorders

Specialized care for various anxiety disorders including generalized anxiety, social anxiety, separation anxiety, and panic disorders.

Medication Management

Careful, evidence-based medication management when appropriate, with close monitoring and adjustment to optimize outcomes.

Collaborative Care

Coordination with therapists, nutritionists, educators, and other healthcare providers to ensure comprehensive, integrated treatment.

The 1-to-1 Approach

At 1-to-1 ADHD & Anxiety Solutions, we believe in providing the same personalized, attentive care that has defined Dr. Nash's practice for over a decade. Our telemedicine platform allows us to extend this commitment to families across California while maintaining the quality and individual attention that our patients deserve.

We understand that navigating ADHD and anxiety challenges can be overwhelming for families. That's why we take the time to listen, understand your concerns, and develop treatment plans that work for your child's unique situation and your family's lifestyle.

Our goal is to help children and adolescents not just manage their symptoms, but truly thrive - maximizing their academic success, social relationships, and overall happiness and wellbeing.
