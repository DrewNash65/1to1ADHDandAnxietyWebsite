Our Approach

At 1-to-1 ADHD & Anxiety Solutions, we believe that there are multiple factors that contribute to attention problems and anxiety in children. Consequently there is not one 'magic pill' that will fix everything. Dr. Nash takes a multidimensional approach to address the patient's nutrition, environment, and self-regulation skills, which can positively affect a child's success and self-confidence. Using a combination of nutritional counselling, evidence based use of supplements, online talk therapy, and biofeedback and mindfulness are instrumental in achieving wellness. These modalities, in addition to judicious use of pharmacologic medications is what allows 1-to-1 ADHD & Anxiety Solutions provide the best and most effective treatment plan to enable your child to thrive, succeed, and be their happiest.

How it works

1

Pre-register by filling out the form below. Do this for each of your children.

2

You will be sent credentials to log in to your patient portal as well as a secure link to enter credit card or HSA card information with our partner Stripe. (We don't collect or keep any of your financial information, ever.)

3

When you would like an appointment, log into the patient portal and select a time that works for you.

4

Your credit card will be charged a $50 deposit to secure the appointment time. (Your appointment is not confirmed until the deposit is paid.)

5

Prior to your appointment, you will be sent a secure link to connect a HIPAA compliant Telehealth plaform. You may use a phone, tablet or computer with a camera and microphone.

6

After the appointment, your card will be charged the balance due (total less deposit paid) and you will be sent a receipt to submit to your insurance company or HSA.
