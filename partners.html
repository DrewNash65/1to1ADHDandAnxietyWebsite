Our Partners

We believe in a comprehensive, multidimensional approach to ADHD and anxiety treatment. That's why we've partnered with leading organizations that share our commitment to improving child wellness through evidence-based solutions.

Supporting Your Child's Success

At 1-to-1 ADHD & Anxiety Solutions, we recognize that comprehensive care extends beyond medications alone. Our carefully selected partners provide complementary services and products that address nutrition, mindfulness, evidence-based digital therapeutics, and targeted supplementation—all key components of our multidimensional treatment philosophy.

Whether your child needs nutritional guidance, mindfulness training, prescription-based digital therapy, or specialized supplementation, our partners are trusted providers committed to your child's wellbeing.

Muse

Mindfulness & Focus Training Device

Muse is a scientifically-designed wearable headband that provides real-time feedback to teach mindfulness and focus skills. It's an excellent complementary tool for children managing ADHD and anxiety.

Through gamified meditations and focus exercises, Muse helps children develop better self-regulation, emotional awareness, and concentration. We recommend this device for age-appropriate patients as part of a comprehensive treatment plan.

EndeavorRx

FDA-Cleared Digital Therapeutic

EndeavorRx is a prescription digital therapeutic that has been FDA-cleared specifically for improving ADHD symptoms in children ages 8-17. It's a clinical-grade game-based treatment that delivers measurable results.

Patients play an engaging adventure game designed to exercise the neural systems that support attention, impulse control, and executive function. This is a evidence-based, prescription-required treatment that can be a valuable addition to a comprehensive ADHD management plan.
