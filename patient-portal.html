Patient Portal

Portal Features

Appointment Scheduling

Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments online. Available time slots are updated in real-time, and you'll receive automatic reminders about upcoming visits.

Document Upload

Securely upload medical records, school evaluations, testing results, and other relevant documents before your appointment for more efficient care.

Secure Messaging

Communicate directly with Dr. Nash about non-urgent questions, medication refills, or follow-up concerns through our secure messaging system.

Treatment Plans

Access your child's treatment plans, progress notes, and care summaries to stay informed about their ADHD and anxiety management.

Medication Management

View current medications, dosages, and prescription information. Request refills and track medication history.

Billing & Payments

View statements, make payments online, and download insurance-ready super bills for out-of-network reimbursement.
