Out-of-Network Care

1-to-1 ADHD & Anxiety Solutions is an out-of-network provider for all commercial insurance plans. This allows us to focus on providing personalized, comprehensive care without the constraints and limitations often imposed by insurance companies.

Payment is expected at the time of service, and we provide all necessary documentation for you to submit claims to your insurance company for potential reimbursement.

Payment Options

HSA/FSA Cards

We accept HSA and FSA cards for payment. We provide detailed billing statements that meet most plan requirements for documentation.

Credit/Debit Cards

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

Insurance Reimbursement

We provide super bills with CPT and ICD-10 codes for you to submit to your insurance company for potential out-of-network reimbursement.

Understanding Out-of-Network Benefits

1

Check Your Coverage

Contact your insurance provider to understand your out-of-network health benefits, including deductibles, co-insurance rates, and reimbursement requirements.

2

Pay for Services

Payment is due at the time of service using your preferred payment method (HSA/FSA, credit card, etc.).

3

Submit Claim

Receive a detailed super bill with all necessary codes and documentation to submit to your insurance company for reimbursement.

4

Receive Reimbursement

Your insurance company processes your claim and provides reimbursement according to your out-of-network benefits.

Questions to Ask Your Insurance Provider:

  • What are my out-of-network health benefits?
  • Do I have a deductible for out-of-network services?
  • What is my co-insurance percentage for out-of-network care?
  • Do I need pre-authorization for telemedicine services?
  • What CPT codes are covered for ADHD and anxiety treatment?
  • How do I submit claims for out-of-network reimbursement?

Good Faith Estimate

Under the No Surprises Act, you have the right to receive a "Good Faith Estimate" explaining how much your medical care will cost. You can ask our office for a Good Faith Estimate before scheduling an appointment.

For questions or more information about your right to a Good Faith Estimate, visit www.cms.gov/nosurprises or call the 1-800-985-3059 helpline.
